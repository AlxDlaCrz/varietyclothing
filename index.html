About Us

Welcome to Variety Clothing, your number one streetwear in Pampanga, a source for all choices of clothing. We are dedicated in providing you our exquisite premium quality products, with an emphasis on high quality fabric, connection with our market , creating our own identity, and consistency. Founded in 2019 by Franz Panlilio, Variety Clothing has come a long way from its beginnings in Angeles City. Franz Panlilio first started out his passion for "Spend less, look good" drove him to start his own business. We hope you enjoy our products as much as we enjoy offering them to you.
